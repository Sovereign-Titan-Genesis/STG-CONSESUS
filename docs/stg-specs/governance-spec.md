1. Purpose

Dokumen ini mendefinisikan:

- governance architecture,
- proposal lifecycle,
- validator governance,
- treasury governance,
- quorum system,
- voting integrity,
- dan sovereign decentralized coordination.

---

2. Governance Objectives

Governance STG dirancang untuk:

- menjaga stabilitas ekosistem,
- mencegah governance abuse,
- mendukung decentralization,
- menjaga treasury accountability,
- dan memastikan long-term coordination sustainability.

---

3. Governance Principles

Governance wajib:

- transparent,
- auditable,
- quorum-based,
- anti-oligarchy,
- validator-compatible,
- dan consensus-safe.

---

4. Governance Scope

Governance mencakup:

- proposal management,
- validator voting,
- treasury approval,
- protocol upgrades,
- emergency coordination,
- dan ecosystem policy management.

---

5. Governance Restrictions

Governance TIDAK boleh:

- centralized,
- opaque,
- treasury-abusive,
- validator-exclusive,
- atau bypass consensus integrity.

---

6. Governance Layers

Governance terdiri dari:

- proposal layer,
- voting layer,
- treasury oversight layer,
- validator governance layer,
- audit layer,
- dan emergency governance layer.

---

7. Governance Participants

Partisipan governance dapat mencakup:

- validators,
- treasury council,
- ecosystem contributors,
- infrastructure maintainers,
- dan governance delegates.

---

9. Proposal Categories

Proposal dapat mencakup:

- protocol upgrade,
- treasury allocation,
- validator policy,

- dan governance amendment.

---

10. Voting System

Voting system wajib:

- deterministic,
- transparent,
- replay-safe,
- auditable,
- dan consensus-compatible.

---

11. Quorum Policy

Quorum digunakan untuk:

- proposal validation,
- treasury execution,
- protocol modification,
- dan emergency governance control.

---

12. Treasury Governance

Treasury governance wajib:

- multi-signature aware,
- auditable,
- reserve-protected,
- dan governance-reviewed.

---

13. Validator Governance

Validator governance mencakup:

- protocol participation,
- consensus voting,
- governance review,
- emergency coordination,
- dan network sustainability.

---

14. Emergency Governance

Emergency governance digunakan untuk:

- security incidents,
- validator compromise,
- treasury protection,
- chain stabilization,
- dan sovereign continuity response.

---

15. Governance Replay Protection

Governance wajib melindungi terhadap:

- duplicate voting,
- replay attack,
- proposal duplication,
- governance spoofing,
- dan signature manipulation.

---

16. Proposal Integrity

Setiap proposal wajib:

- timestamped,
- cryptographically signed,
- explorer-visible,
- governance-auditable,
- dan immutable.

---

17. Governance Transparency

Seluruh governance activity wajib:

- publicly reviewable,
- audit-compatible,
- event logged,
- dan consensus-verifiable.

---

18. Governance Auditability

Governance system wajib mendukung:

- forensic review,
- proposal traceability,
- validator accountability,
- dan treasury verification.

---

19. AI Governance Boundaries

STG-AI diperbolehkan:

- membaca governance telemetry,
- mendeteksi anomaly,
- menghasilkan diagnostics,
- memberi recommendation.

STG-AI TIDAK diperbolehkan:

- melakukan voting,
- mengontrol treasury,
- mem-bypass quorum,
- atau melakukan autonomous governance execution.

---

20. Governance Security Model

Governance dirancang untuk memitigasi:

- governance capture,
- validator cartelization,
- treasury abuse,
- quorum manipulation,
- dan centralized control escalation.

---

21. Upgrade Governance

Protocol upgrade wajib:

- proposal-based,
- quorum-approved,
- validator-reviewed,
- audit-logged,
- dan rollback-aware.

---

22. Emergency Override Restrictions

Emergency override wajib:

- time-locked,
- multi-signature protected,
- audit-visible,
- governance-reviewable,
- dan consensus-compatible.

---

23. Governance Event Logging

Event governance wajib mencatat:

- proposal creation,
- voting activity,
- quorum validation,
- execution state,
- dan treasury interaction.
