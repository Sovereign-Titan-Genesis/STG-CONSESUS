1. Purpose

Dokumen ini mendefinisikan:

- arsitektur ekonomi STG,
- distribusi token,
- validator incentives,
- treasury sustainability,
- governance economy,
- dan long-term sovereign digital economy model.

---

2. Economic Objectives

Ekonomi STG dirancang untuk:

- menjaga sustainability jaringan,
- memberi insentif validator,
- mendukung governance participation,
- menjaga treasury resilience,
- memitigasi economic collapse,
- dan memastikan long-term ecosystem continuity.

---

3. Economic Principles

Tokenomics wajib:

- transparent,
- auditable,
- anti-fragile,
- inflation-aware,
- sustainability-oriented,
- dan governance-compatible.

---

4. Scope

Ekonomi STG mencakup:

- native token issuance,
- validator rewards,
- treasury reserve,
- governance incentives,
- ecosystem development,
- dan sovereign reserve sustainability.

---

5. Restrictions

Ekonomi TIDAK boleh:

- hyperinflationary,
- opaque,
- governance-abusive,
- validator-exploitative,
- atau treasury-unsustainable.

23. AI Economic Boundaries

- mencetak token,

- atau memegang sovereign reserve authority.
